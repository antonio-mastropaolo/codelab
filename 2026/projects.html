Five concept platforms built with generative AI tools. Each showcases what undergrad students can ship when armed with modern LLMs and a bit of ambition.

What is Vibe Coding?

Building real software primarily through natural language — describing what you want, letting AI generate the implementation, and iterating fast. These projects showcase what undergrad students can ship in a single semester when armed with modern LLMs and a clear vision.
