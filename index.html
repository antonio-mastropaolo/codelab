About

The Course & Platform

CODELAB is an experiential learning course at William & Mary focused on building real-world software using modern AI-assisted development practices.

Students are challenged to ship working products — not just write code — by exploring emerging tools, design practices, and the intersection of human creativity with AI capabilities. This platform serves as a living record of that work: teaching modules, student projects, and resources are added throughout each semester.

The course emphasizes vibe coding, rapid prototyping, and the kind of software creation that was previously reserved for experienced teams — now accessible to any student willing to experiment.
